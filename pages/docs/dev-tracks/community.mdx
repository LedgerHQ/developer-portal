# Get in touch with the Ledger developer community

Joining Discord is not required to code your <Glossary word="Device Application"></Glossary>, but it will be easier to meet with our team and discuss the specifics of your project.

Join us on the [Ledger's Discord server](https://developers.ledger.com/discord/) and introduce your project in the
**#dev-support** channel.

The following channels might interest you:

- [dev-support](https://discord.com/channels/885256081289379850/1007621744732950590) is the place for technical question for developers. 
- [bitcoin](https://discord.com/channels/885256081289379850/910840532283039764) for all things related to Bitcoin development.
- [embedded-app-updates](https://discord.com/channels/885256081289379850/907623554542080070) is the channel the device app developers use to communicate about SDK releases, Boilerplate evolutions, or anything which could impact our development environment or tools.
- [rust-sdk](https://discord.com/channels/885256081289379850/912241185677000704) is a channel dedicated to the applications using the WIP Rust SDK.
- [blockchain-foundation](https://discord.com/channels/885256081289379850/907623688759803935) is the support channel for all issues related to adding currency support on Ledger Live.
