# Companion Wallet

A companion wallet is your own app that is fully compatible with your <Glossary word="Device App"></Glossary> and will allow your users to make transactions on your chain. 

Requirements:

- The wallet gives an option to verify the receiving address on a Ledger device. 
- The wallet has an <Glossary word="affiliate link"></Glossary> next to the "Connect with Ledger" option.
- The wallet has GUI running on Windows/MacOS/Linux
